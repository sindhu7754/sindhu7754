<h3 align="center">a little about me</h3>

I'm a CS graduate who never quite settled on one field, because the universe — literally and figuratively — kept giving me more to be curious about. My work lives mostly in **distributed systems, cybersecurity, and machine learning**, but my reading list lives in astronomy, oceanography, and geology. I like systems that fail gracefully, signals that hide in noise, and questions that don't have tidy answers yet.

- 🛰️ Building fault-tolerant systems inspired by how satellites talk to each other across gaps in connectivity
- 🔐 Digging through network logs and IDS rules for the anomaly that shouldn't be there
- 🌊 Reading about ocean currents and plate tectonics purely because the scale of them fascinates me
- 📡 Best Paper Award, ICCCSP 2026 · Paper accepted, IEEE SPICES 2026
- 🧭 Currently exploring: contact-graph routing, low-resource ASR, and how far "curiosity-driven" can take a career
